How Did We Come To Be?
Surprisingly the idea of Sophicopy wasn't as a result of a research nor was it carefully planned. It came suddenly when I needed to write a landing page copy for a digital product I wanted to sell. I realized I didn't have a business name yet so I created one. And with that Sophicopy was born.
Here's the full story
In August 2021, I received a text message from my elder sister. She told me about her decision to enroll me in an online whatsapp class where I could learn a skill called copywriting. I was a bit annoyed cause then I just got started in this business world. I had a lot of free classes I joined that needed my attention. But I had no other choice cause my sister made used of her own money to pay the fee for me. Towards the end of the class we were given a project to do that will help in testing our skills and getting better. I did my project at the last minute but it still yielded some results and I got some people to buy what I sold. So while I was doing the project, I thought I needed a professional business name. And it came easily to me though - the name Sophicopy.
How The Name "Sophicopy"
Was Created
Like I said in the homepage, I create brand names and it really comes easy to me, more like my second nature. Example of some of the names I created are: Sophicopy, Sophi-Info, Mavix Glow, Medivo, Health Med Hub and many others. I hardly think too much about it, but the one name that I and my graphic designer researched so much about before creating was a very special work. The name was Mavix Glow. But not to take too much of your time, let me do a brief summary of how I got the name - Sophicopy. I just sat down and thought of what name to use, I decided to try out a brand name created from cut-out parts of a name. So I cut out the "a" part of my name Sophia and added "copy" gotten from copywriting, joined both of them together. And voila we have a brand new brand name.
